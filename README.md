# 📊 Customer Segmentation & Behavior Analysis (Alfido Tech)

## 🔍 Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze customer transaction data to identify meaningful customer segments, understand purchasing behavior, and detect potential churn risks. This helps businesses make data-driven decisions to improve customer engagement and retention.

---

## 📁 Dataset

The dataset contains customer transaction details including:

* Customer ID
* Purchase Date
* Product/Transaction details
* Quantity & Pricing

---

## ⚙️ Techniques Used

### 🧹 Data Cleaning

* Removed duplicate records
* Handled missing values
* Converted date columns to proper datetime format

### ⚙️ Feature Engineering

* Created **TotalAmount** (Quantity × Price)
* Extracted time-based features

---

## 👥 Customer Segmentation

### 📌 RFM Analysis

* **Recency (R):** Days since last purchase
* **Frequency (F):** Number of purchases
* **Monetary (M):** Total spending

### 🤖 K-Means Clustering

* Customers grouped into 4 segments:

  * High Value Customers 💎
  * Loyal Customers 🔁
  * At Risk Customers ⚠️
  * Low Value Customers 📉

---

## 📊 Visualizations

* 📈 Monthly Sales Trend
* 📊 Customer Retention Distribution
* 🔥 Correlation Heatmap
* 📍 Customer Cluster Visualization

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* A small group of customers contributes a large portion of revenue
* Many customers make only one purchase → high churn risk
* Strong relationship between purchase frequency and revenue
* Seasonal trends observed in monthly sales

---

## 💡 Recommendations

1. 🎯 Introduce loyalty programs for high-value customers
2. 📧 Re-engage inactive customers with targeted campaigns
3. 🎁 Offer discounts to customers at risk of churn
4. 🛒 Use personalized recommendations to increase sales
5. 📅 Plan seasonal marketing strategies based on trends
